Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is renowned for its art, fashion, culture, and gastronomy. Known as the "City of Light," Paris is a global center for art and culture, with iconic landmarks and world-class museums.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower - The iconic symbol of Paris offering breathtaking views of the city.
  2. Louvre Museum - The world's largest art museum and a historic monument in Paris.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées - One of the most famous avenues in the world, known for its theatres, cafés, and luxury shops.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica - A stunning basilica located at the highest point in the city, offering panoramic views.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants - Flaky, buttery pastries that are a staple of French breakfasts.
  • Baguette - A long, thin loaf of French bread that is crisp on the outside and soft on the inside.
  • Escargot - A French delicacy of cooked land snails, usually served as an appetizer.
  • Ratatouille - A traditional Provençal stewed vegetable dish.
  • Macarons - Sweet meringue-based confection made with egg white, icing sugar, granulated sugar, almond meal, and food coloring.
